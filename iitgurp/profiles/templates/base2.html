शोध, IIT Guwahati

The aim of शोध is to connect faculty who require assistance on their research projects with interested members of the student community. The approach has been to minimise the barrier of usability in order to promote undergraduate and interdepartmental research collaborations.

Guided by the Director, Dean SA and Chairman, Students' Welfare Board.
